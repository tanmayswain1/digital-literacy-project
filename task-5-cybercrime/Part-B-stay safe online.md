# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify calls claiming to be from banks or officials
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Never scan a QR code to receive money (it is used for payment)
- Always double-check payment requests before approving
- Do not install unknown apps suggested by callers

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in

# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## Do's
- Think before posting any content online
- Maintain a respectful and positive tone
- Protect your personal information and privacy
- Verify information before sharing it
- Use social media for learning and networking

## Don'ts
- Do not share offensive or inappropriate content
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information
- Do not engage in online arguments or negativity
- Avoid oversharing personal details
- Do not misuse others' content without permission
